Our Story

Moueene was founded with a simple mission: to make finding reliable service providers as easy as possible. We believe everyone deserves access to quality services for their homes and families.

What started as a small idea in 2020 has grown into a thriving community of thousands of service providers and satisfied customers. Our platform bridges the gap between people who need help and skilled professionals who can provide it.

Today, Moueene is the leading platform for home services, connecting families with trusted childcare providers, tutors, cleaners, gardeners, and more. We're proud to have helped over 25,000 customers find the perfect service provider for their needs.

Our Mission

To create a world where finding trustworthy service providers is simple, safe, and accessible to everyone. We strive to empower both customers and providers by building a platform based on trust, quality, and convenience.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for all home and personal service needs, setting the standard for quality and reliability in the service industry.
