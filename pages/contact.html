Common Questions

Quick answers to your most frequent questions

How do I book a service?
Simply browse our categories or search for a specific service. Select a provider based on reviews and price, choose a time slot, and confirm your booking. It's that easy!
How are providers verified?
We have a strict verification process including identity checks, background screening, and skill verification. Only qualified professionals make it to our platform.
What if I'm not satisfied?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with a service, contact our support within 24 hours, and we'll work to make it right or offer a refund.
How do payments work?
We use secure payment processing. You pay through the app when you book, but funds are only released to the provider after the job is completed to your satisfaction.
