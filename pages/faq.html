General Questions

What is Moueene?
Moueene is a trusted platform that connects customers with professional service providers. We offer a wide range of services including childcare, tutoring, cleaning, gardening, pet care, and elderly care. Our platform makes it easy to find, book, and pay for quality services.
Is Moueene free to use?
Yes! Creating an account and browsing services is completely free for customers. You only pay for the services you book. Service providers pay a small commission fee on completed bookings.
How do I create an account?
Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button, choose whether you want to register as a customer or service provider, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.

Booking & Services

How do I book a service?
To book a service: 1) Browse or search for the service you need, 2) Select a provider and review their profile, 3) Choose your preferred date and time, 4) Confirm your booking and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.
Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by provider. Generally, cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Payments & Security

How does payment work?
All payments are processed securely through our platform. We accept credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Payment is held securely until the service is completed, then released to the provider. This protects both customers and providers.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full credit card details. Our payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers that are PCI DSS compliant.

For Service Providers

How do I become a service provider?
To become a provider: 1) Sign up and select "Provider" account type, 2) Complete your profile with your services, experience, and rates, 3) Submit required documents for verification, 4) Once approved, you can start receiving booking requests.
