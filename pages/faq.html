General Questions

What is Moueene?

Moueene is a trusted platform that connects customers with professional service providers. We offer a wide range of services including childcare, tutoring, cleaning, gardening, pet care, and elderly care. Our platform makes it easy to find, book, and pay for quality services.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button, choose whether you want to register as a customer or service provider, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

Is Moueene free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing services is completely free for customers. You only pay for the services you book. Service providers pay a small commission fee on completed bookings.

Booking & Services

How do I book a service?

To book a service: 1) Browse or search for the service you need, 2) Select a provider and review their profile, 3) Choose your preferred date and time, 4) Confirm your booking and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by provider. Generally, cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. Always check the provider's specific policy before booking.

What if the provider doesn't show up?

If a provider fails to show up for a confirmed booking, please contact our support team immediately. We'll help you find an alternative provider and issue a full refund. We take reliability seriously and repeated no-shows may result in the provider being removed from our platform.

Payment & Security

How does payment work?

All payments are processed securely through our platform. We accept credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Payment is held securely until the service is completed, then released to the provider. This protects both customers and providers.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full credit card details. Our payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers (Stripe/PayPal) that are PCI DSS compliant. Your financial information is always protected.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy and the specific provider's terms. If you're eligible for a refund, it will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. For disputes, contact our support team.

For Service Providers

How do I become a service provider?

To become a provider: 1) Sign up and select "Provider" account type, 2) Complete your profile with your services, experience, and rates, 3) Submit required documents for verification, 4) Once approved, you can start receiving booking requests. The process typically takes 2-3 business days.

What fees does Moueene charge providers?

Moueene charges a small service fee of 15% on completed bookings. This covers payment processing, platform maintenance, customer support, and marketing. There are no monthly fees or hidden charges - you only pay when you earn.

How do I get paid?

Payments are released to your account within 24-48 hours after a service is completed and confirmed by the customer. You can withdraw funds to your bank account or PayPal. We process payouts weekly on Mondays.

Trust & Safety

Are service providers verified?

Yes! All service providers undergo a verification process that includes identity verification, background checks, and credential verification (where applicable). Look for the "Verified" badge on provider profiles to identify fully verified providers.

What if I have a problem with a service?

If you experience any issues, first try to resolve it directly with the provider through our messaging system. If that doesn't work, contact our customer support team. We have a dispute resolution process and will work to find a fair solution for all parties.

How can I report a problem or safety concern?

You can report issues through your booking page, the provider's profile, or by contacting our support team directly. For urgent safety concerns, please call our emergency hotline. We take all reports seriously and investigate thoroughly.
