Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a service?

Simply browse our services or providers, select the one that meets your needs, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Are the service providers verified?

Yes! All providers on Moueene go through a verification process including identity verification and background checks. Look for the "Verified" badge on provider profiles.

How does payment work?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. You can pay with credit card, debit card, or PayPal. Payment is only released to the provider after the service is completed.

What if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by provider, but generally, cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund.
