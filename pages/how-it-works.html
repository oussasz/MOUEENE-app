Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to join?

Yes, signing up for Moueene is completely free for both customers and providers. We only charge a small service fee on completed bookings.

How are providers verified?

We perform identity checks, review certifications, and conduct background screenings for all our verified providers to ensure your safety.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, contact our support team within 24 hours, and we'll help resolve the issue.
