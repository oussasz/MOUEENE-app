About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah, a passionate childcare specialist with over 10 years of experience working with children of all ages. I hold a degree in Early Childhood Education and multiple certifications in child development and first aid.

I believe in creating a nurturing, safe, and fun environment where children can learn, play, and grow. My approach combines structured activities with free play to support children's physical, emotional, and cognitive development.

I'm experienced in caring for infants, toddlers, and school-age children. I can help with homework, prepare nutritious meals, and organize engaging activities. References are available upon request.
